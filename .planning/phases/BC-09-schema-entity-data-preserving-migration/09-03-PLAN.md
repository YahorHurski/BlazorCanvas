<objective>
Swap the production stack onto the D-59 anchor+geometry model in one coherent move: rewrite the `Figure` entity and `CanvasDbContext` mapping, then adapt the three readers the change forces — `FigureStore`, `SyncMessage`, and `Home.razor` — using `GeometryCodec` (09-01) as the single Box<->anchor+geometry seam. The goal is a production assembly that COMPILES and behaves identically to v1.1 on the new storage, WITHOUT pulling in Phase 10's behavioural rework (no clamp removal, no drag-updates-only-x,y, no sync-payload rework).

This is the build-continuity plan 09-CONTEXT.md flags: changing the entity breaks every reader of `X1..Y2` and of the integer `Id`. We resolve it by adapting read paths minimally — the app keeps working in `Box` in-memory and converts at the persistence/broadcast boundary — so the phase can end build-green with current behaviour intact.

Purpose: land the model change with zero behavioural drift; leave the running app on the new schema shape.
Output: rewritten entity + DbContext mapping; adapted FigureStore, SyncMessage, Home.razor.
